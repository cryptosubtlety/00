# How to learn zero-knowledge proof (ZKP)?

There are many tutorials, videos, podcasts about ZKP which may be overwhelming. Here is my selected list in _specific order_:

1. [Computer Scientist Explains One Concept in 5 Levels of Difficulty | WIRED](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fOGdb1CTu5c)
2. [What is a SNARK? by Dan Boneh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h-94UhJLeck)
3. [Building a SNARK, pt 1 by Dan Boneh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J4pVTamUBvU)
4. [IP#2 - Sumcheck Protocol by Alessandro Chiesa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s7WRHfqLy8g&list=PLGkwtcB-DfpzST-medFVvrKhinZisfluC&index=3)
5. [IOP based Zero-Knowledge Proofs by Alessandro Chiesa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q1-iAAUFy0Y)
6. [KZG polynomial commitments by Dankrad Feist](https://dankradfeist.de/ethereum/2020/06/16/kate-polynomial-commitments.html)
7. [Polynomial Commitments by Justin Drakes, 3 parts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bz16BURH_u8)
8. [ZKdocs by trailofbits](https://www.zkdocs.com/)
9. [The PLONK zero-knowledge proof system by David Wong, multiple parts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RUZcam_jrz0&list=PLBJMt6zV1c7Gh9Utg-Vng2V6EYVidTFCC)
10. [Building a SNARK, pt 2 by Dan Boneh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vxyoPM2m7Yg)
11. [Zero Knowledge from the Discrete Logarithm Problem by Dan Boneh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hO6feBcKQ98)
12. [Coordinated disclosure of vulnerabilities affecting Girault, Bulletproofs, and PlonK by trailofbits](https://blog.trailofbits.com/2022/04/13/part-1-coordinated-disclosure-of-vulnerabilities-affecting-girault-bulletproofs-and-plonk/)
13. [Anatomy of a STARK by Alan Szepieniec](https://aszepieniec.github.io/stark-anatomy/)
